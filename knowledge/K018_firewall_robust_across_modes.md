# K018 — The firewall is robust across modes: a characterization, not a wall met once

> **Explainer** (`GOVERNANCE.md`). Self-contained; the project's own use cited by `B`/`V`/`P` number (no re-proof);
> standard material cited to the literature. Nothing here promotes to `../CLAIMS.md`; never a premise in a proof.

The 2026-06 program asked, in three structurally different ways, whether a **dimensionful physical scale** can
emerge from the project's object. All three say no — and they say it the *same* way: the scale is **external**, the
wall **relocates** but never dissolves. This explainer consolidates that convergence (the headline of the
forward-frontiers push) so it reads as one result, not five.

## The object, in one line (the category root, P010)

The object is the **Betti side** of the non-abelian Hodge correspondence — the moduli space of *flat* connections
on the once-punctured torus, with its trace-map dynamics. It is **kinematic, scale-free, topological**: it has
*form* (towers, spectra, monodromies, symmetries), not *content* (a running coupling, a scale, a time, a chosen
vacuum, fields). Every result below is an instance of that one fact.

**One verdict:** the scale is external in *every* reachable mode. The firewall is not a wall met once and patched;
it is the **same wall reached by three independent routes** — a characterization of what a flat-connection
moduli-space object structurally lacks. (`B151`/`P010` named it; B167/B168/B169 tested it in the invariant,
dynamical, and ensemble modes; all relocate.)

## The honest boundary (the firewall, in proper terms)

The mathematics sits **exactly on** real (emergent + symmetry) physics; the **crossing** to the dimensionful,
*selected* universe is proven-blocked **from within**, in every mode tested. The path to physics is therefore a
**bridge + a relocated wall**, not a crossing — the strongest honest statement the program supports. What remains
genuinely open is *external* by the same analysis: the Higgs/Hitchin scale (NEEDS-SPECIALIST), the off-axis Cantor
theorem (NEEDS-SPECIALIST), and one untested speculative leap — whether the accretion *growth-rate itself* could be
