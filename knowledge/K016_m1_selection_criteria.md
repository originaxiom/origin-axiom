## The family and the question

The metallic once-punctured-torus bundles `M_m = R^m L^m` (`K002`/`K004`) form a one-parameter family. The figure-eight
(`m=1`) is repeatedly singled out. The criteria below are independent characterizations; together they make `m=1` the
*most-selected* point of the family (not the *only* special one — arithmeticity, e.g., is a two-element selector).

## The criteria

**Classical (geometric / arithmetic):**

1. **Systole — smallest volume.** `m=1` is the figure-eight, the minimal-volume orientable cusped hyperbolic
   3-manifold; the systole / volume is extremal in the family. (B107; `K009`.) The volume coincidence with the
   sister `m003` (P9/P10) is **cross-family and torsion-broken**: `m003` carries `ℤ/5` torsion (not a positive-word
   `b++` bundle), so **among torsion-free / within `b++` the figure-eight is the *unique* volume minimum** (`B197` —
   sharpens P10's volume filter; still leans on the torsion-free condition, not an independent proof).
2. **Expansion threshold — `λ_m < 2` only for `m=1`.** The metallic mean `λ_m=(m+√(m²+4))/2` is below 2 (the
   quantum-dimension range) only for `m=1`; so only the golden case can be a `2cos` quantum dimension. (B127; `K009`.)
3. **Arithmeticity (a two-element selector).** `m=1` (ℚ(√−3)) is arithmetic; so is `m=2` (ℚ(i)); `m≥3` are not. So
   arithmeticity selects `{m=1, m=2}`, with `m=1` the most-selected overall. (B125; `K009`, corrects B123.)

**Quantum (SU(2)_k WRT data — new, B132):**

4. **Pure phase — `|Z_k| = 1` at every non-vanishing level is `m=1`-unique.** The figure-eight's SU(2)_k partition
   function has unit modulus at every level (perfect coherence); all other `m` have `|Z_k|` varying with `k`. The
   strongest single selection criterion. (B132/V121.)
5. **Self-referential loop — `Z_{k=4}(M_1) = ω`.** At the saturation level `k=4`, the partition function outputs
   `ω = e^{2πi/3}`, the generator of the trace field ℚ(√−3): the quantum theory at saturation *outputs its own
   arithmetic generator*. `m=1`-specific. (B132/V121.)

**A `{m=1, m=2}`-pair criterion:**

6. **κ rational on the geometric component (`κ-degree = 1`).** Over ℚ(z) on the geometric component of the bundle
   character variety, `κ = tr[a,b]` is rational (degree 1) for `m=1,2` and acquires higher degree for `m≥3`. (B126;
   the κ-degree sequence `[1,1,3,3,7,6]`.) *(Scope note: an AI-assisted claim that the non-metallic `RRL` (ℚ(√−7)) bundle has
   κ-degree 3 — purportedly refuting "κ-degree=1 ⟺ arithmetic" — did **not** reproduce (B132 found degree 1 or 2,
   convention-dependent) and is **not** banked; the criterion as stated for the metallic family stands.)*

## What it does not license

These select `m=1` *within the metallic family*; they do not make the figure-eight "physical." The selection is a
mathematical distinction (extremal geometry, arithmeticity, quantum coherence). The native physics of the quantum layer
remains the emergent Lee–Yang edge (`K015`, `S030`), not fundamental physics.
