\begin{abstract}
The Origin Axiom introduces a dynamical constraint forbidding perfect global cancellation of complex scalar field amplitudes ($|A| > 0$), addressing the fundamental question of why the universe contains something rather than nothing. Through systematic 3D lattice simulations progressing from static to FRW-expanding universes with self-interactions, we demonstrate the axiom selectively prevents destructive interference with minimal energy injection and no backreaction on expansion. This toy model provides a novel mechanism for vacuum selection and qualitative emergence of positive vacuum energy, fully reproducible via public code at \url{github.com/originaxiom/origin-axiom}.
\end{abstract}
