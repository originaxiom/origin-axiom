quantity*. **Do not** bank a specific match as support.

*structural* claim `TESTED-NEGATIVE`. "Hierarchies are powers of one scale" stays `SUPPORTED` even if matching a
specific hierarchy fails — the structure and the value are different claims at different tiers.
