family

```text

phi_m(b)=a
```

on the standard rank-two `SL(3,C)` trace generators:

```text

x4 = tr(A^-1)       x5 = tr(B^-1)       x6 = tr(A^-1 B)
```

`tr(A^k B)` and `tr(A^-k B^-1)`.
