## Result

The central-sign action is Poisson for the Fricke-Goldman bracket, so the
quotient is compatible with the natural character-variety Poisson structure.

The half-step trace map is anti-Poisson and the `A`-level map is Poisson, as in
B21. This behavior also respects the quotient coordinates.

## Control

The Poisson structure descends for all invariant surfaces `I=const`. It does
not privilege `I=1/4`.
