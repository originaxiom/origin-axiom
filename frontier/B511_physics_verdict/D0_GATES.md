## The four targets, operationalized (structure only — never values)
- **T-SIG (Lorentzian (3,1)):** a natural pairing/quadratic form arising in a door computation whose
  signature is COMPUTED (not asserted) to be (3,1) — with controls showing (n,0)/(2,2)/other in
  comparable generic settings. "Natural" = defined without reference to the target.
- **T-GAUGE (nonabelian gauge in 4d):** must pass the distinction table: gauge (local, gauged bosons)
  ≠ flavor (global) ≠ Galois (arithmetic symmetry) ≠ fusion-space unitaries. A candidate must supply
  the GAUGING mechanism, not a group label. (The no-go T-NOGO-DGG stands: 3d–3d route closed.)
- **T-GEN (forced ×3):** a 3-fold structure that survives the D5 foreign-control battery (the same
  pipeline must NOT produce "a forced d" for d = 4, 5, 7 controls) and is functorially tied to a
  matter-like object, not to a count.
- **T-SCALE (one dimensionful ratio):** a forced ratio of two INDEPENDENT rates already proved exact
  (e.g. drift constants, entropies, resonance rates) that matches a measured dimensionless ratio at
  the committed statistical standard. Matched single numbers are banned (the 14-kill lesson).
