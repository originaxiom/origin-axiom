# B770 PREREGISTRATION — THE CLOSURE CENSUS (Phase 0 of the Closure Program)

*Sealed before any classification runs. The census assigns every open item in the
record one of six states under the rules below. The courier's tier list is ONE
input, diffed against the sweep — never the frame.*

## The six states (four terminal + two working)
- **CLOSED** — the item's question has a banked two-outcome answer with a
  compute-grade lock; nothing remains to run. Requires the discriminating arc
  citation (B-number + where the lock lives).
- **WALLED** — a banked no-go theorem (or proven E-class obstruction) says why it
  cannot close; the wall itself is the deliverable. Requires the wall's citation.
- **CONSTITUTIVELY-OPEN** — the openness is itself proven structure (torsor
  choices, Galois non-canonicity, axiom prices); enters the chain as boundary,
  not backlog. Requires the structural theorem's citation.
- **EXTERNAL** — in-sandbox computation exhausted (the compute-before-deferring
  rule satisfied); carries a registered criterion for the specialist.
- **LIVE** — computable now; assigned a phase (1 mechanical / 2 structural /
  3 correspondence / 4 walls / 5 capstone).
- **STALE** — superseded or duplicated by a later banked arc; requires the
  superseding citation.

## Classification rules (binding)
1. Every non-LIVE verdict cites its discriminating banked fact; the classifier
   VERIFIES the citation exists and says what is claimed (open the file).
2. No discriminating fact in hand ⇒ default LIVE. An unearned CLOSED is the
   census's numerology — the B525 lesson pointed at closure itself.
3. Every CLOSED/WALLED/STALE verdict gets an adversarial second pass ("find the
   residual open part; try to refute the closure"). A verdict that fails drops
   to LIVE with the refutation recorded.
4. Duplicates across ledgers merge to one item carrying all source pointers.
5. Gate 5 and Gate 5-Q apply verbatim; the census banks structure, no values,
   nothing to CLAIMS; the one-number pin is untouchable by any census verdict.
6. The courier's tier list enters as seed items like any ledger; divergences
   between their claimed status and the sweep's verdict are reported explicitly.
