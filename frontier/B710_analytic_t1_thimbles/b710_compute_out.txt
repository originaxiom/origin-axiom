PART 3  Qualitative Stokes / thimble connectivity
==============================================================================
fig-8 state integral:  1-dim oscillatory integral (Andersen-Kashaev Teichmuller TQFT).
  2 non-abelian saddles, actions +/- Vol on the REAL action-axis, symmetric about 0 under
  the conjugation Z/2 whose FIXED POINT (CS=0) is where they live.  The steepest-descent
  contour picks the geometric (dominant) saddle; its conjugate is anti-Stokes-related.
FRW lapse integral:  1-dim oscillatory integral over the lapse N (Picard-Lefschetz,
  Feldbrugge-Lehners-Turok).  4 saddles OFF the real axis (complex actions); the physical
  contour deformation selects 2-of-4, and WHICH 2 is contour-dependent (Lorentzian vs
  no-boundary) -- the crux of the FLT vs Hartle-Hawking Stokes debate.
SHARED: both are 1-dim Picard-Lefschetz/steepest-descent integrals carrying a conjugation
  Z/2 on their saddle sets.  DIFFERS: (#saddles 2 vs 4; mod-conj orbits 1 vs 2), (actions
  REAL vs COMPLEX), (fig-8 peacock lattice has no FRW analogue), (fig-8 saddles sit AT the
  conjugation fixed point CS=0; FRW saddles sit OFF it).
