## The design (lemma-primary, per the red-team fix)

- **PRIMARY — the closure lemma (candidate proof):** every level-k invariant of the word channel
  is a trace of a word in the two generators; by the SL(2) Fricke/Cayley–Hamilton trace
  identities (tr(UV) = tr(U)tr(V) − tr(UV⁻¹)), the trace of ANY word lies in **ℤ[x, y, z]** —
  the ring generated by the seed traces. Induction on word length; one lemma kills all levels.
  Consequence, stated in advance: at the object's point the level invariants live in the seed's
  field forever — **the word channel cannot leave its birth arithmetic** — and the lemma is
  manifestly CLASS-GENERIC (it holds for every 2-generator word system): closure is the
  environment's property, not the organism's distinction.
- **SECONDARY — the bounded counterexample scan (L = 8, precommitted):** compute the actual
  per-level invariant sets (subword traces, their minimal polynomials/fields; the per-level
  κ-data) for levels ≤ 8 and verify each lands in the seed ring/field. Any non-whitelist
  structure fires the escalation bin.
- **Per-level choice analysis:** at each level the substitution continuation is deterministic —
  verify no step admits an inequivalent canonical continuation (the B449 method per round); the
  chain-level answer is banked (choice enters at L57).
