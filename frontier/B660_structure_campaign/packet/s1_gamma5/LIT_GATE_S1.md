## 1. THE MODEL SPACE (pattern-space denominator)

Finite modular groups Γ_N = SL(2,Z)/Γ(N) used as lepton-flavor symmetries, plus double/other covers Γ'_N, with the earliest/key paper establishing each as a flavor symmetry:

| Group | Iso. | Order | Key paper |
|---|---|---|---|
| Γ2 | S3 | 6 | Kobayashi-Shimizu-Takagi-Tanimoto-Tatsuishi, [1906.10341](https://arxiv.org/abs/1906.10341) |
| Γ3 | A4 | 12 | Feruglio, "Are neutrino masses modular forms?", [1706.08749](https://arxiv.org/abs/1706.08749) (2017, foundational) |
| Γ4 | S4 | 24 | Penedo-Petcov, [1806.11040](https://arxiv.org/abs/1806.11040) |
| Γ5 | A5 | 60 | Novichkov-Penedo-Petcov-Titov, [1812.02158](https://arxiv.org/abs/1812.02158), JHEP 04 (2019) 174 |
| Γ6 | S3×A4 | 72 | Li-Liu-Ding, [2108.02181](https://arxiv.org/abs/2108.02181) |
| Γ7 | PSL(2,7) | 168 | Ding-King-Li-Zhou, [2004.12662](https://arxiv.org/abs/2004.12662) |
| Γ'3 | T′ | 24 | Liu-Ding, [1907.01488](https://arxiv.org/abs/1907.01488) |
| Γ'4 | S4′ | 48 | Novichkov-Penedo-Petcov, [2006.03058](https://arxiv.org/abs/2006.03058); also Liu-Yao-Ding, [2006.10722](https://arxiv.org/abs/2006.10722) |
| Γ'5 | A5′ = SL(2,5) | 120 | Wang-Yu-Zhou, [2010.10159](https://arxiv.org/abs/2010.10159) |
| Γ'6 | — | 144 | Li-Liu-Ding, [2108.02181](https://arxiv.org/abs/2108.02181) |

(Γ'2 coincides with Γ2 since −I ∈ Γ(2), so N=2 has no distinct double cover.) Non-canonical but citable extensions of the same program: binary octahedral 2O (Ding-Liu-Lu-Weng, [2307.14926](https://arxiv.org/abs/2307.14926)); binary dihedral 2D3 (Arriaga-Osante–Liu–Ramos-Sánchez, [2311.10136](https://arxiv.org/abs/2311.10136)); metaplectic cover of A5 (Yao-Liu-Ding, [2011.03501](https://arxiv.org/abs/2011.03501)); "multiple modular symmetries" combining several Γ_N at once, non-count-additive (de Medeiros Varzielas-King-Zhou, [1906.02208](https://arxiv.org/abs/1906.02208)).

**Count: 10 distinct groups in the strict Γ_N/Γ'_N ladder (N=2..7, 6 unprimed + 4 primed); 13 if 2O, 2D3, and the metaplectic A5 cover are included.** Reviews: Feruglio [1706.08749](https://arxiv.org/abs/1706.08749); Kobayashi-Tanimoto, "Modular flavor symmetric models," [2307.03384](https://arxiv.org/abs/2307.03384); Ding-King, "Neutrino Mass and Mixing with Modular Symmetry," [2311.09282](https://arxiv.org/abs/2311.09282), Rept. Prog. Phys. 87 (2024) 084201 — title/abstract confirmed directly; abstract states it gives "detailed results for N=2,3,4,5" plus eclectic-flavor top-down discussion. No verbatim "master count" sentence was found in either review (PDF full-text extraction failed); the count above is derived from surveying the primary papers, not quoted from a review.

## 2. THE A5/A5′ SUBSPACE — papers, forms, reps, free choices, sin²θ12

Fully verified (full text read), Γ5≃A5: **Novichkov-Penedo-Petcov-Titov** [1812.02158](https://arxiv.org/abs/1812.02158) — builds the weight-2 level-5 basis (11 forms → 3⊕3′⊕5 of A5) plus higher weights; L~3′; two Weinberg-operator models: wt-2 best fit sin²θ12=0.3252 (poor θ13 fit); wt-4 (Z2^S stabilizer, τ=i) sin²θ12=0.2824 (viable 1.7σ). **Ding-King-Liu** [1903.12588](https://arxiv.org/abs/1903.12588) — 16 model classes (A1–A8/B1–B8), L~3 or 3′, charged leptons singlets; model A1 sin²θ12=0.2937; model A3 sin²θ12=0.3109. **de Medeiros Varzielas-Lourenço**, "Two A5 modular symmetries for Golden Ratio 2 mixing," [2206.14869](https://arxiv.org/abs/2206.14869) — two independent A5 moduli, stabilizers τ_l=i∞ (T-fixed) and τ_ν=i (S-fixed); sin²θ12≈0.2826.

Γ5′≃A5′=SL(2,5): **Wang-Yu-Zhou** [2010.10159](https://arxiv.org/abs/2010.10159) — first level-5 weight-1 A5′ forms; L~3, RH neutrinos in the genuinely new spinorial doublet **2′**; minimal seesaw, general fit sin²θ12≈0.3047 (sub-cases 0.301–0.332). **Yao-Liu-Ding** [2011.03501](https://arxiv.org/abs/2011.03501) — full A5′ weight-1–6 construction, 720 candidate models scanned, 25(49) viable NO(IO); best-fit sin²θ12 clusters tightly ≈0.290–0.306 across viable models; metaplectic-cover benchmark sin²θ12=0.30398. Also (abstract-verified only, details not independently confirmed): Criado-Feruglio-King [1908.11867](https://arxiv.org/abs/1908.11867); Wang-Zhou [2102.04358](https://arxiv.org/abs/2102.04358); Behera-Mohanta [2201.10429](https://arxiv.org/abs/2201.10429), [2108.01059](https://arxiv.org/abs/2108.01059); Li-Lu-Ding [2410.24103](https://arxiv.org/abs/2410.24103); Li-Ding [2509.15183](https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.15183) (non-holomorphic A5/A5′, Maaß forms).

**Free choices across this literature, consistently**: modular weight assignment per multiplet, A5(′) irrep assignment for L/E^c/N^c (3, 3′, singlets, or the A5′-only 2′), Kähler potential (minimal, assumed not derived), the flavon sector and its own transformation/alignment when not τ-only, and the ⟨τ⟩ vacuum value (usually fit to data, occasionally fixed at a symmetric/stabilizer point). sin²θ12 best-fit values across all models found range ≈0.28–0.33, i.e. straddle but do not single out any specific closed-form value.

## 3. THE SPECIFIC-VALUE CHECK — sin²θ12=(√5−1)/4

**Not found anywhere as an exact prediction.** (√5−1)/4=1/(2φ)=cos(2π/5)≈0.309017 is a genuine A5 character-table quantity (1/φ=2cos(2π/5) appears in the A5 character table, King-Luhn review [1104.0055](https://arxiv.org/abs/1104.0055)) but no paper identifies it with sin²θ12. It sits numerically between the two real named "golden ratio mixing" values from the pre-modular A5-flavon literature: **GR1** (cotθ12=φ; Kajiyama-Raidal-Strumia [0705.4559](https://arxiv.org/abs/0705.4559); A5-realized in Ding-Everett-Stuart [1110.1688](https://arxiv.org/abs/1110.1688)) gives sin²θ12=(5−√5)/10≈0.2764; **GR2** (cosθ12=φ/2, i.e. θ12=36°; Adulpravitchai-Blum-Rodejohann [0903.0531](https://arxiv.org/abs/0903.0531); A5-realized in Everett-Stuart [0812.1057](https://arxiv.org/abs/0812.1057)) gives sin²θ12=(5−√5)/8≈0.3455 — confirmed arithmetic: cosθ12=φ/2 IS a real character-table-derived quantity (φ/2=cos36°), stated precisely, and it is NOT equal to 1/(2φ). A less-standard "GR3" (cosθ12=φ/√3) is catalogued in Ballett-King-Luhn-Pascoli-Schmidt [1410.7573](https://arxiv.org/abs/1410.7573), sin²θ12≈0.127, excluded. **These are pre-modular (flavon, not modular-form) A5 constructions**, not part of the Γ5/Γ5′ modular literature proper.

## 4. THE FIVE-TONE SET {0, 1/(2φ), 1/2, φ/2, 1}

**Not found as a named/standard set.** Components co-occur informally: Ding-Everett-Stuart [1110.1688] literally lists |Ue1|=φ/2 and |Uμ1|=1/(2φ) side by side; 0/½/1 are ubiquitous trivial limits (θ13=0, maximal θ23, unit norm). No source treats the five values as a defined structural/character object.

## 5. RESIDUAL SYMMETRY & GEOMETRIC DERIVATION

De Medeiros Varzielas-Levy-Zhou, "Symmetries and stabilisers in modular invariant flavour models," [2008.05329](https://arxiv.org/abs/2008.05329) proves every Γ_N stabilizer is **cyclic** — Z2×Z2 can never arise as a modular stabilizer (only in pre-modular flavon A5 models, which do use Z2×Z2 for neutrinos alongside Z5 for charged leptons — a real divergence between the two paradigms). For Γ5: T (order 5)→Z5 at τ=i∞; S·T (order 3)→Z3 at τ=ω; S (order 2)→Z2 at τ=i, each with further Γ5-inequivalent points.

Geometric/string derivation of the flavor group itself: magnetized-brane program (Kobayashi-Tanaka-Tatsuishi [1803.10391](https://arxiv.org/abs/1803.10391); Kobayashi et al. [1804.06644](https://arxiv.org/abs/1804.06644), [1811.11384](https://arxiv.org/abs/1811.11384)) and eclectic-flavor program (Baur-Nilles-Trautner-Vaudrevange [1901.03251](https://arxiv.org/abs/1901.03251); Nilles-Ramos-Sánchez-Vaudrevange [2006.03059](https://arxiv.org/abs/2006.03059), [2010.13798](https://arxiv.org/abs/2010.13798); Baur et al. [2008.07534](https://arxiv.org/abs/2008.07534), [2104.03981](https://arxiv.org/abs/2104.03981), [2207.10677](https://arxiv.org/abs/2207.10677)) have concretely derived S3, D4, A4/T′, S4/S4′, Δ(96), Δ(384) from orbifold/flux geometry, and Kikuchi-Kobayashi-Otsuka-Takada-Uchida [2007.06188](https://arxiv.org/abs/2007.06188) explicitly derives S4′ from magnetized-torus flux. **A5/SL(2,5) is at most an abstract classification entry** in this geometric program (Kikuchi-Kobayashi-Uchida [2101.00826](https://arxiv.org/abs/2101.00826) lists N=5 among mathematically-allowed covers) **with no worked, phenomenologically-developed geometric construction found anywhere** — every A5/A5′ flavor paper (§2) postulates the group by hand.

## 6. BOTTOM LINE

**(a)** Pattern-space count: **10** groups in the strict Γ_N/Γ'_N ladder (N=2–7 unprimed + N=3,4,5,6 primed); **13** with 2O/2D3/metaplectic-A5 extensions.
**(b)** sin²θ12=(√5−1)/4: **NO** — not found as an exact prediction anywhere; it is a genuine A5-adjacent character-table quantity but is never used for θ12 (GR2's cosθ12=φ/2 is the real, distinct, cited relation).
**(c)** Most load-bearing citations to check first: Feruglio [1706.08749](https://arxiv.org/abs/1706.08749) (founds the whole program); Novichkov-Penedo-Petcov-Titov [1812.02158](https://arxiv.org/abs/1812.02158) (founds Γ5=A5); Wang-Yu-Zhou [2010.10159](https://arxiv.org/abs/2010.10159) (founds Γ5′=SL(2,5)); Ding-King review [2311.09282](https://arxiv.org/abs/2311.09282) for context.
