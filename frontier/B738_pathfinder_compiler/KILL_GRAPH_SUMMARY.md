### Empty / near-empty face-columns — the report

**The emittance columns are ~empty, as expected.** B735 postdates essentially the entire corpus:

- `emittance-lengths` — 2 records total (**B335**, **B486**), and in both the length spectrum was touched
  only to CONFIRM a symmetry / a CM-point identification, never mined as the discriminating channel.
  No kill in the corpus has ever used the geodesic length spectrum as its decisive fact.
- `emittance-eigenvalues` — 3 records total (**B239**, **B247**, **B285**), all eigenvalue-ADJACENT
  arithmetic (trace/disc menus, holonomy eigenvalues), none the object's Laplace/scattering spectrum.
  The eigenvalue/resonance face proper (Maass spectrum, cusp resonances) has been consulted by
  **zero** kills — it is a fully virgin column. Every `native-continuous-channel` hatch in the corpus
  (21 records) points at exactly this pair of columns.
- `infinite-hecke` — 4 records (**B693**, **B698**, **B734**, **B736**), all from the last two weeks;
  B693 itself flags that only depth-one of the Hecke structure was ever consulted.
- Also thin: `coupled-double` (16) and `meeting` (17) relative to `being` (112) — the corpus is heavily
  solo-object-weighted; and the `none` rows (10) are kills that consulted no object face at all
  (pure value-scans, intake adjudications, meta-kills).
- By kill-form: `value-numerology` and `zero-intertwiner` never once consulted an emittance or
  infinite-hecke column; `finite-truncation` (2 rows) never consulted any continuous face.

**Reading**: the corpus's negatives overwhelmingly interrogated the object's discrete/algebraic faces.
The two emittance columns plus the infinite-hecke column are the structurally under-consulted directions,
which is exactly where the pathfinder's Candidate Zero composition sits.
