## Result

A bounded projective census finds fixed points and the coordinate-axis
period-3 family. The B26 orbit is the simplest nontrivial one-parameter
projective family:

```text
(0,0,c) -> (c,0,0) -> (0,c,0) -> (0,0,-c)
```

But the family is parametrized by `c`; it does not select `I=1/4`.
