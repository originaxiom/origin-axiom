# P013 — The structural theorem is a Galois theorem

> **Philosophy** (`GOVERNANCE.md`): an interpretation of banked mathematics, one-way (math → philosophy), never a
> premise in a proof, nothing promoted to `../CLAIMS.md`. The companion to `P001` (architecture, not furniture) and

## The claim

`P001` observed that the object speaks **architecture** (symmetry, shape, possibility) and is silent on **furniture**
(values, units, real-time). That was stated as a *finding* — "the sort is the finding." This entry upgrades it to a
**mechanism**: the silence on furniture is not an empirical gap that further looking might close; it is the consequence
of a Galois symmetry the object's own arithmetic carries. **Every discrete invariant of the object is a Galois orbit of
its own arithmetic Galois group — so choosing a value is choosing an arithmetic *labeling*, never forcing a physical
*value*.** The value-free monad (`P009`) is, precisely, a Galois theorem.

## Why this is the right frame

A "forced choice" (`K013`/B130) would be an invariant that is (1) invariant, (2) discretely multivalued, and (3)
*unsymmetrizable* — multiple genuinely-distinct values the structure's own symmetry cannot relate. The program checked
where such a thing could live and found it nowhere:

- **The continuous invariants don't choose.** `κ` is free on the fixed locus (B130) — a continuum, not a discrete set.
- **The discrete invariants are Galois orbits.** Where the object *does* produce discrete numbers — the CP sign
  `±π/6` on the Eisenstein end (B285), the colored-Jones / modular data on the golden end (B314) — the multiple values
  are exchanged by the Galois action of the object's own trace field. They fail condition (3) *by an algebraic symmetry
  the object carries*, not by accident.

The object is **two-ended** (`ℚ(√−3)` Eisenstein, `ℚ(√5)` golden; B261), and **each end carries its own Galois `ℤ/2`**:
`√−3 → −√−3` symmetrizes the classical/CP data, `√5 → −√5` (`φ → −1/φ`) symmetrizes the quantum/WRT data. The `±` of the
CP phase and the `1∓√5` of the colored Jones are the *same kind of object* — a Galois conjugate pair, not a fork.

## What it means

The structural theorem stops being a list of "we looked and found no value" and becomes a single structural statement:
**a value is something a Galois orbit cannot be.** A coupling, a sign, a scale, a count — each would have to be a
*distinguished* element of a discrete set; but the object's arithmetic makes every such set a single orbit, with no
distinguished element. This is why "the object forces form, not values" recurs identically across all four faces of `κ`
(`P008` coda; existence/geometry/matter/quantum) and across the upstream member-selection: it is one theorem with one
cause.

This **strengthens the firewall** rather than relaxing it. `GOVERNANCE §2` ("we have the form, not the contents") is no
longer only a discipline we impose; it is a property the object provably has. The honest residual is the *general*
version (`S032-A`, `OPEN_LEADS` L7): that **no** invariant whatsoever — not just the classical (trace ring, B130) and
quantum (Face IV, B314) classes, but any cohomological or torsion invariant — escapes the symmetrization. Two of the
three load-bearing classes are sealed; the all-invariants theorem is the named open target, and the Galois mechanism is
its candidate proof. If it closes, single-seed member-contingency (`P000`) is **irreducible**, and the only door to a
genuine choice is **heterogeneity** — gluing two *distinct* seeds (`K014`/B131), never one.
