## The reading

The trace map's Jacobian at a hyperbolic fixed point is **reciprocal-closed** `(λ,1/λ)`, and time-reversal — iterating
the inverse map — acts as the involution `λ↔1/λ`, swapping the stable and unstable manifolds (B124, generic
symplectic). The proposed reading: the two mirror manifolds are **two directions of time**, with a central
low-entropy reference point (the fixed point) and observers on either branch each calling their own (expanding)
direction "forward." This is the **Carroll–Chen** picture (entropy increasing in *both* directions from a central
minimum) and resonates with the **CPT-symmetric universe** (Boyle–Finn–Turok): the microscopic laws are ~T/CPT-
symmetric, and the experienced arrow is thermodynamic (the Past Hypothesis), not dynamical.

## Why it is the most *natural* fit — and still only an overlay

What recommends this reading over earlier ones is that it **uses the object's reversibility instead of fighting it.**
We had flagged reversibility as the *obstacle* to deriving an arrow (`../speculations/S002`); the two-headed reading
**dissolves** that problem rather than solving it — there is no arrow to derive, only a reference point and two
directions, and reversibility becomes the *signature* of two-directional time. It also fits the **proved** mode count
(B124): the spectrum is **exactly forward/backward symmetric at every rank** (expanding count `==` contracting
count) — there is **no arrow**, exactly as a two-headed picture requires. A genuinely better fit than any
arrow-*producing* overlay.

But better-motivated is not load-bearing. Three fences, all standing:

1. **It is an overlay, not structure** — the same tier as the fenced matter/geometry readings; the `λ↔1/λ` involution
   and the reciprocal pairing are a structurally real connection to **T-symmetry**, which makes the reading apt, not
   true. It lives here (`philosophy/`), labeled; never in `../knowledge/`.
2. **Generic, not metallic-specific** — this is the decisive fence, our own lesson applied without exception for
   beauty. *Every* symplectomorphism is two-faced; reciprocity is symplectic geometry's property, not our object's
   (B124 §1). The only metallic-specific residue is **handedness** (the det=−1 chirality asymmetry, P-not-T, B124

   (does the seed select the reference point?).
3. **Firewall** — the map's "time" is iteration-count of a monodromy on a moduli space, **not** physical/cosmological
   time. The Carroll–Chen resonance is a structural analogy between two reversible systems, not a claim that the
   trace map *is* cosmic time. Same type mismatch (moduli dynamics vs 4D spacetime) that killed the physics bridges.

## The corrected core (what the math actually says)

The spectrum's only metallic-specific asymmetry is a **chirality (P)** imbalance, **not** a time-direction (T) arrow:
`−w₀ =` contragredient `=` character-level time-reversal, and its `±1` eigenspaces are time-reversal **even**/**odd**;
an excess of one is symmetric between the two directions. So the two-headed reading stays **exactly symmetric — no
arrow** — and what is metallic-specific is *handedness*, not a chosen time direction (B124, correcting an earlier
"asymmetry = arrow" temptation).
