# P005 — Laws and states: infinite structural depth, finite fundamental content

## The stance

The degree=rank result **splits** (B120/Q2), and the split has a clean reading:

- **(I) spectral degree=rank is a LAW.** `char(Mⁿ)` is a tower factor ⟺ `μ_n=1`, and `μ_n=1` for **all `n`**. It
  holds at every rank, with no exceptions — the kind of statement that is true of the *structure itself*, not of
  any particular instance.
- **(II) geometric degree=rank is a STATE.** The longitude `= meridianⁿ` at an irreducible boundary-unipotent rep,
  and that rep **exists only for `n ∈ {3,4}`** (B95/B119: the forced principal spectrum degenerates at n=5 and is
  absent at n≥6). It is a feature of particular low-rank *instances*, not of the structure.

So the same phenomenon is a **law** on one reading and a **state** on the other: **infinite structural depth (the
law holds at every rank), finite fundamental content (the geometric realization lives only in two ranks).**

## Why this is worth saying

It is the project's own version of a familiar shape: *the rules are universal, the realizations are not*. The
tower's organizing law (the Sym two-sequence, `char(Mⁿ)` always present) runs up every rank — `P001`'s
"architecture of the possible." The geometric degree=rank — an actual irreducible Dehn-filling representation with
`L = M^n` — is a **state of the world** that only two ranks happen to admit. Banking negatives (`P003`) taught us
to read the absence as information; here the absence (no irreducible principal for n≥5) is exactly what separates
the *law* from the *state*.

This pairs with `P004` (the universality is interaction-born) and `B120`'s **m-universality / physics-distinctness**
contrast (the algebra is one law; the metallic materials are distinct states): the program keeps finding the same
duality — one structure, many instances; a universal grammar, particular sentences. That is the architecture, not
the furniture.

## What it does not license

This is a renaming of "all-n vs `n∈{3,4}`" in the vocabulary of laws and states. It asserts **no** 3d-3d dictionary,
no physical interpretation of "law" beyond the mathematical sense (a statement true at every rank). The geometric
content really does stop at n=4 (B119); calling it a "state" does not extend it.
