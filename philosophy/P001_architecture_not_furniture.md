# P001 — Architecture of the possible, not the furniture of the actual

> **Philosophy — motivation only** (`GOVERNANCE.md`). Not a claim; never a premise. Cites the mathematics one-way.

## The insight

The framework is a theory of **the space of possibilities and its symmetries** — not of any particular contents
filling that space. The character variety is the moduli space of vacua (the 3d-3d dictionary), equivalently a
quasicrystal's spectral-RG configuration space. Its "dynamics" are **scale (renormalization), not time**; its
content is generated by **one number `φ`** in all its powers (`⊕ Symᵈ(M)`).

So the framework speaks about **architecture** — symmetry, hierarchy, the *shape* of the vacuum, the *direction*
of flow, topology — and is structurally **silent** about **furniture** — specific dimensionful values (a
cosmological-constant number, particular masses), units, and real-time evolution. Moduli-space monodromy carries
neither operator content nor units.

## Why this is the load-bearing stance

It **pre-sorts every question** before any computation:

- A question about *structure* → the framework can speak → it ends in a **calculation pointer**.
- A question about a *value* or *real-time dynamics* → the framework is silent → the honest answer is a **wall**.

This is not a post-hoc excuse for the walls; it is a *prediction about where the walls will be*, and the
speculation catalog confirms it — every strong (`SUPPORTED`/`ACTIVE`) entry is structural, every dead/held entry
asks for a value (`../speculations/CATALOG.md`). **The sort is the finding.**

## The discipline it enforces

P001 is why two lines are never crossed (`../speculations/GOVERNANCE.md`): **numerology** (matching a value) and
**tower-eigenvalues = masses** (reading monodromy as operator content) are both demands for *furniture* from a
theory of *architecture*. The bravery is preserved precisely by knowing which kind of question one is asking.
