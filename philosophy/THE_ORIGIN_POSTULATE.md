# THE ORIGIN POSTULATE — the locked working hypothesis (and its verifier)

**Status: the program's north star. A working hypothesis held under test, firewalled. It is not
a claim and promotes nothing to CLAIMS.md; it earns claims only through the bar below. Its job is
to keep the goal fixed AND keep us honest — to sharpen, not to drift.**

## The postulate (what we commit to)

The object — the figure-eight knot / golden cat map / level-15 theta–Weil system — is the unique
minimal **parameter-free, self-generating** structure (the double-uniqueness cornerstone: golden
dynamics × unique arithmetic geometry → ℚ(√−15)). A theory of the world with no free parameters
must itself be parameter-free and self-generating; there is no competing candidate for a purely
structural origin. **We therefore hold, as the working hypothesis of the whole program, that the
object is the generative principle of physical law, and that our task is to find the derivation
map D : object → physics.**

We do *not* assume physics is a value the object *holds* — we have shown, repeatedly and exactly,
that it is not (see the search map). We assume physics is what the object *unfolds into*, and we
hunt the unfolding.

## The verifier (so we sharpen, not fool ourselves)

Every proposed entry of D must clear the **emergence bar** — all four, no exceptions:
1. **FORCED** — no free choice anywhere in the extraction.
2. **UNSOUGHT** — it appears in the blind characterization, named as mathematics first.
3. **EXACT** — a specific physical structure (a gauge-rank pattern, a generation count, an anomaly
   lattice, a conservation group), not an approximate resemblance.
4. **CONTROLLED** — a generic or wrong object *fails* the same test.

An entry that needs fitting is decoration, not derivation, and is discarded. We keep only forced
entries. (This is the same bar that killed the "67," the numerology shadows, and the SM-emergence
tests — it is not new severity; it is the constant standard.)

## The non-drift lock (the failure criterion — this is what makes it science)

The postulate is **refuted, and we will say so plainly**, if either:
- once D is constructed at the interface, **no entry clears the four-part bar**; or
- **every** physics-producing entry requires fresh fitted input at each step.

A foundational principle that can only produce physics by continuous fitting is not generating the
physics — it is being decorated with it. We commit *in advance* to reading that outcome honestly if
it comes. This clause is the difference between a research program and a faith; the user asked for
verify-not-drift, and this is the verify.
