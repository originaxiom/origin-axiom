# `philosophy/` — governance

This folder holds the project's **philosophical motivation** — the layer *behind* the speculations. Where
`../speculations/` logs readings that end in a *calculation pointer*, `philosophy/` holds the positions that
motivate the whole enterprise and need not point at any calculation at all.

> **The one rule, absolute:** *philosophy is motivation, never premise, never claim.* Nothing here promotes to
> `../CLAIMS.md`; nothing here may appear as a premise, lemma, or step in any proof, `../frontier/` FINDINGS,
> `../papers/` note, ledger row, or commit message. The arrow is **one-way**: a philosophy doc *may* cite a proved
> result (`B`/`V`/`P` number) to say "this is the mathematics it motivates"; **the mathematics never cites
> philosophy.** If any sentence here ever needs to be cited to *support* a mathematical claim, the boundary has
> been crossed — stop, and either restate the content as neutral mathematics in `../frontier/`, or it does not
> belong in the proof at all.

Consistent with root `../GOVERNANCE.md`: **§5** (nothing promotes to the proven core without passing the gates;
`dead` is permanent), **§8** (anti-overclaim wording — "a Lorentzian form in phase space," never "spacetime"; "`A`
is the figure-eight monodromy," never "the universe is a knot"), **§9** (append-only `PROGRESS_LOG.md`; `CLAIMS.md`
is the only living claim ledger).

## Contents

- `PHILOSOPHICAL_PATHS.md` — the **P1–P5** register of *non-mathematizable* arguments for "nothing → something"
  (linguistic, Gödelian, anthropic, process, apophatic). A path leaves this register only by being mathematized
  (gaining an `E`-tag).
- `METALLIC_FOUNDATIONS.md` — *why aim the theorem at the family, not the seed* (the rationale behind the metallic
  classification; the source material for P000).
- `P000`–`P003` — the **foundational layer** (a *different axis* from P1–P5):
  - **P000** — *what is not-nothing?* (the four chosen premises; the family-not-a-seed shape).
  - **P001** — *architecture of the possible, not the furniture of the actual* (the organizing insight).
  - **P002** — *necessity given chosen premises* — framed explicitly as **a position one may hold**, NOT a derived
    truth (saying "the universe exists by mathematical necessity" would violate this very governance and contradict
    P000's irreducibility-of-choice).
  - **P003** — *dead-ends as boundaries* ("bank negatives" elevated to a philosophical stance).

## The discipline, once more

The original Origin Axiom intuition is philosophical *before* it is mathematical, so excluding philosophy would be
dishonest; but letting it mingle with claim-bearing work would collapse the discipline of `../GOVERNANCE.md`.
Separation preserves both. This folder is where the *why* lives — argued, never computed, and clearly outside the
claims layer.
