## The root problem, in one sentence

Every firewall result is the same fact in a different costume: **the object is the character variety — the moduli
space of *flat* connections on the once-punctured torus — and that is the wrong *category* to contain
dimensionful, dynamical, selected physics.** It has **form** (towers, spectra, monodromies, symmetries) but not
**content** (a running coupling, a scale, a time, a chosen vacuum, fields). Physics is downstream of *dynamics
that breaks scale-invariance*; a moduli space of flat connections is, by construction, kinematic, scale-free, and
topological.

## The flaw in the original question

The Origin Axiom asked whether non-cancellation **derives the contents of existence** — Λ, masses, constants. But
a character variety is, by its nature, the **space of possibilities**, not a selection within it (`P001`: the
architecture of the possible, not the furniture of the actual; B130/`K013`: a moduli space *parametrizes*, it
does not *choose*). The question asked a parametrizing object to be a selecting-and-scaling one. That is a
**category error**, and naming it is the point of this entry. The honest reframe — the one the B161–B163 arc
actually executes — keeps the object in its category: it describes **order within existence** (why the residue
cannot be nothing: non-cancellation, the Cantor structure), not the **furniture** of existence.

## The one new lens: Betti (flat) vs Hitchin (dynamical)

The six obstructions above unify under one geometric statement. The object lives on the **Betti side** of the
non-abelian Hodge correspondence — flat connections, character variety, *kinematic and topological*. The
**dynamical, scale-carrying structure lives on the Hitchin / Higgs (Dolbeault) side** — a hyperkähler integrable
system with a genuine flow (`S024` already noted the tower sits "on the monodromy side of Hitchin"). The bridge
between the two sides is **isomonodromy / Painlevé VI**, which the seed's natural partner — the **4-punctured
sphere**, the only other dim-2 Fricke cubic (`OPEN_LEADS` H5-c) — carries, and whose dynamical degree the program
already cites (B150, Cantat–Loray). So the missing "dynamics" is not nowhere: it is **on the other side of a
correspondence the program has only touched.**

field's**, which in the class-S / 3d–3d dictionary is the *gauge theory's* scale — **external** (B151). So crossing
to Hitchin most likely **relocates** the firewall (the Painlevé "time" is a dimensionless modulus, not physical
time — the same type-mismatch as `P006`) rather than dissolving it. It is the one frontier where the object grows
a genuine dynamics. **(Update — this lens has since been RUN: B164 built the (0,4) Fricke cubic + the Painlevé-VI/MCG
dynamics; B169 ran the isomonodromy flow, computed the dynamical degree `λ_m²`, and confirmed the dimensionless-time

## The constructive flip (so the consolidation is not only a wall)

The same results that close the *crossing* show the object reached the **maximal extent of a kinematic / symmetry
object**: it **is** the symmetry side of a real physical theory (the SL(2,ℤ) trace-map action **is** the N=2\*
class-S S-duality mapping-class action — FORCED, B150/L14), and it **is** a real emergent system (the metallic
Schrödinger cocycle / Fibonacci quasicrystal — `K007`/`K010`, the aperiodic-order physics that is observed in the
lab). The program's mathematics sits **exactly on** real (emergent + symmetry) physics. What it does not do — and
has proven it cannot, from within — is *generate* the dimensionful, selected, dynamical side. That is the honest
shape of "the path to physics": a real bridge to the physics that is already there, and a proven wall before the
physics that would have to be *chosen and scaled* into being.
