## The provocation (owner, 2026-06-30)
*"Our framework is dynamical, not static. The evolution from distinction to all this math is dynamic — why do we
need dynamics externally?"* Correct, and it exposes a sloppy "the object is static" framing. The object is **not**
static. The fix is to separate three things the word "dynamics" conflates — only one is external, and for a precise
reason.

## Three senses of "dynamics"
1. **Generative unfolding** — distinction → `ℚ(√−3)` → 2T → E₆ (B266/B282). Real, internal, *and it is exactly what
   we compute*. But it is **atemporal entailment**: `ℚ(√−3)` is not *later than* distinction, it is *entailed* by it
   (cf. the Mandelbrot set "generating" detail — all present at once, not produced in time).
2. **The object's internal recursions** — the AJ q-difference equation (B261), the trace map (K008/B124), the WRT
   level-period (B204), the RG/scale flow (`P001`). These are **genuine dynamical systems** — in an *abstract*
   parameter (color `N`, iteration count, level `k`), **not** physical time (`P006`: iteration ≠ cosmic time).
3. **Physical temporal evolution** — a flow in physical *time*, with a rate, an arrow, and a *selection* of which
   outcome is actual. **This** is the only external one, and it splits into (a) and (b):

## (a) Selecting an asymmetric outcome is external by Curie's principle — a wall, not a preference
The object is **symmetric**: CP-symmetric (B252), amphichiral, and its spectrum is forward/backward symmetric — *no
arrow* (`P006`/B124). **Curie's principle:** a symmetric structure under a symmetric dynamics yields symmetric
effects — symmetry in, symmetry out. So matter-over-antimatter, a handedness, a chosen vacuum (all **asymmetric**)
**cannot** be produced by the object's own internal dynamics, however dynamical, because that dynamics preserves the
object's symmetry. The dynamics generates the **space** of possibilities — the τ-fork (B271), the two ends, the
±π/6 phase (B285) — *that* is architecture, and it is internal. What it cannot do is **pick one**. Picking is
symmetry-breaking, and a symmetry-preserving evolution cannot break a symmetry from the inside.

**This is the mechanism `P001` was missing.** P001 observed the sort (structure → speakable, value/dynamics → wall)
and said "the sort is the finding." Curie says *why* the sort is forced: **actualization = selecting the actual from
the symmetric space of the possible, and a symmetric object provably cannot select.** Even spontaneous breaking only
delivers the *manifold* of vacua (structure); *which* vacuum is realized is fixed by history/fluctuation — outside
the symmetric law. Architecture-not-furniture is therefore a **theorem-shaped** stance, not a posture.

## (b) The scale/initial-condition is where "external" may dissolve — the live fork
"Why `k ~ 10¹²²` now" is **not** a symmetry-breaking question — it is a rate + an initial condition. And here the
object may supply the dynamics *internally*: it carries a **symplectic structure** (the Goldman bracket on the
character variety; the NZ symplectic frame B262) — a Hamiltonian/conjugate-pair skeleton — and **Alexander–Magueijo–
Smolin 2018** (arXiv:1807.01381, verified this session) makes physical **time emerge as the variable conjugate to
`Λ`** (Chern–Simons time). So *"physical time emerges from the object's own symplectic structure, and `k(t)` is the
flow along it"* is a real, sharp, possibly-computable hook — precisely `../speculations/S002`'s open fork ("does the
seed select the reference point?"). **Caveat (firewall):** a conjugate pair is a *Hamiltonian structure*, not a
*solved trajectory* (2018 gives conjugacy + uncertainty, not `k(t)`); and a flow still needs an initial condition
(the "age") to locate *now*. So this is a frontier, not a settled internalization.

## The corrected core
- **Generative + scale/iteration dynamics: INTERNAL.** "The object is static" is retracted.
- **Actualization-by-selection (the sign, the vacuum, chirality): EXTERNAL, and necessarily so** — Curie forbids a
  symmetric object from selecting an asymmetric actual.
- **The initial condition / emergent time (the scale, `k(t)`): GENUINELY OPEN** — possibly internal via the object's
  symplectic structure (S002); not yet shown.

So "why external?" → *not* because dynamics is external (it isn't), but because **actualization is** — and
actualization is two things: a symmetry-breaking selection (a hard wall) and a where-are-we-now initial condition
(an open frontier). The structural theorem stands, now with its mechanism.
