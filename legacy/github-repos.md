`github.com/originaxiom` organization. As of 2026-05-22 they are **superseded by this
consolidated repository**. The plan (pending org-admin authentication) is to archive each
as read-only and add a "superseded by" pointer to its README — they are **kept, not
deleted**: their commit timestamps are the project's tamper-evident record of progression.
