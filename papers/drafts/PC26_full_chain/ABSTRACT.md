# PC26 — Abstract and significance

## Title

**Chirality from the Figure-Eight Knot: the θ-Odd Sector of the E₆ Character
Variety and Its Listeners**

(Registry alias: "The Standard Model's Structure from the Figure-Eight Knot,"
PC26. The draft title states what is proved; the alias states the motivation,
which is firewalled to §9.)

## Abstract

Let ρ be the principal composition of the discrete-faithful holonomy of the
figure-eight knot complement into E₆(ℂ), and let θ be the diagram involution
(the E₆ → F₄ fold), which a companion paper identifies with the knot's own
hyperelliptic involution on the deformation complex. This paper proves that
the θ-grading of the deformation space is a *chirality* grading, and develops
the measurement theory of that chirality. First, the deformation theory at ρ
is unobstructed through third order — the quadratic form H¹×H¹ → H² vanishes
identically (21 components, exact over ℚ(√−3)) and the Massey triple products
vanish exactly — by two independent computational pipelines that agree on all
components. Second, the variety is chirality-blind exactly on the θ-even
(F₄-stable) stratum and chiral along every θ-odd deformation: every sl₂-stable
subalgebra is a block sum, θ-even sums close in f₄, and any activation of a
θ-odd block forces the full e₆; the associated twisted mirror-double
representation has Zariski closure E₆(ℂ), so its 27 is genuinely complex.
Third, three unhearability theorems: the vacuum is conjugation-fixed and
[C,S] = [C,T] = 0, so vacuum and Dehn-filling probes never detect the θ-odd
sector at any level; and for every color of every stage, a bare knot state has
J_V = J_{V̄} (invertibility plus duality), so single-object states are θ-even
identically. Fourth, the minimal nontrivial listener is the antiphase mirror
channel (e_λ − e_λ̄)/√2, with the operational identity tr_odd = ½(Z − Z_C);
the mirror-twisted play is the play of the other SL(2,ℤ) lift of the
monodromy, so chirality is precisely what the two lifts agree on; and the
chiral and achiral channels add in quadrature (no linear interference), the
one-loop phase dichotomy being the sign law of the six twisted torsions.
Fifth, on the SU(3) tower the chiral amplitude obeys the exact two-tone law
tr_odd = [4|κ] − [5|κ]/φ, derived from a Weyl-twisted finite Weil
factorization into twelve Gauss sums whose conductors are det(A⊗(±w) − I);
on the golden stage (κ = 5) and on E₆ level 2 the *entire* invariant is
θ-odd, with per-pair amplitudes given exactly by the ℤ/7 sine kernel times
14th roots of unity. Whether a given stage hears the object's √5 voice as
symmetric or chiral is decided by whether −1 lies in the stage's Weyl group —
the same Gauss sum exchanges parity sectors under level-rank. All results
carry explicit status labels; the five structural walls (values, selection,
index, compactness, reachability) are restated as non-claims.

## Significance statement (3 sentences)

This paper upgrades "the figure-eight knot forces the E₆ → F₄ fold" to a
complete chirality theory: the θ-odd deformations are the chiral degrees of
freedom, they exist (unobstructed to third order), they force the full
exceptional algebra with a genuinely complex 27, and they are invisible to
every single-object probe while being fully audible — indeed, on special
stages carrying the whole invariant — to an observer holding the object and
its mirror in antiphase. The amplitude laws are exact arithmetic (two-tone
divisibility with golden coefficient, twelve Gauss sums, the ℤ/7 sine
kernel), and the parity of what is heard is a property of the listening
theater's symmetry group rather than of the object. The paper claims
structure only: the walls separating this mathematics from physics are
restated as theorems-of-absence, and the coupling-thesis motivation is
confined to a firewalled closing section.
