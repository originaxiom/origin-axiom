*Owner's question: which papers carry REAL contribution, not paper-for-the-
sake-of-it? Ranked by the only thing that matters — whether a new, checkable
result survives a literature gate — not by how finished the prose is.*

**The governing fact:** the registry (PC01–PC22) is already honest. Most
existing cards are flagged "component theorems KNOWN; only the assembly is
candidate-novel; NEEDS-SPECIALIST." That is not a defect — it is the firewall
working on the papers too. It also means the true count of "clean new theorem"
candidates is SMALL. Below is that small set, plus what each actually needs.

## Tier 2 — real, but narrower or newly-scoped (2)

**The reconstruction / observer-flow note (B535 + B540).** "A primitive
substitution is reconstructible from any single derived-system read-out plus
its grammar, uniquely up to conjugacy; the derived systems form a finite flow
whose only attracting-basin fixed point is the substitution itself." Clean
symbolic dynamics, program-INDEPENDENT (states without a word of physics),
genuinely novel-in-framing. **Gate:** the Durand derived-sequence / Host
rigidity literature — the pieces are established; the one-measurement /
finite-flow framing may be new. A real Ergodic Theory & Dynamical Systems
note if the framing clears. Not yet drafted; strongest *untapped* candidate.

**The degree-4 gap-label proposal (B542/B543/B546).** NOT a theorem paper —
the module theorem is CLASSICAL (BBG 1992, confirmed by the lit-gate). It is
an EXPERIMENTAL PROPOSAL: the first buildable chain whose measured gap labels
realize a degree-4 (ℚ(√φ)) module, at 4×10⁻⁷. Different genre (a physics
letter / proposal), narrower claim (instantiation + experiment, not the
theorem). **Gate:** lit-gate pass 2 (is there ANY prior degree-4 example?),
in flight. If none → the novelty is the realization + the proposal.

## Tier 3 — consolidation / assembly-novelty (the PC01–PC21 bulk)

Survey and consolidation cards whose load-bearing math is known (Falbel–
Guilloux dimension counts, Slansky E₆, CIZ modular invariants, Coste–Gannon
Galois action, Cantat rigidity). Real intellectual assembly, but the theorems
are cited, not new. Publishable — if at all — as expository/survey, and only
after the NEEDS-SPECIALIST reads. Do NOT dress these as contributions; that is
the paper-for-paper's-sake failure mode the owner named.

## Tier 4 — should NOT be written as contributions

- Anything gated on Whang / BGS where prior art likely owns it (the classical
  phantom, B537/B545) — interesting, but not ours to claim until the gate runs.
- Any physics-interpretation paper — the reframe's ledger is that no physics
  claim survives; a physics paper would be an overclaim by construction.
- Any repackage of a banked negative as a positive.

## The one honest meta-rule

A paper is worth writing iff a *new, checkable* statement survives its
literature gate — the same discipline as CLAIMS.md, applied to prose. By that
rule the program's genuine paper output is: **PC22 (gate pending), P4 (patches
pending), the reconstruction note (undrafted, gate pending), and the degree-4
proposal (gate in flight)** — four real candidates, each one lit-search away
from a yes/no. Everything else is consolidation until a specialist says
otherwise. Quantity is not the goal; surviving the gate is.
