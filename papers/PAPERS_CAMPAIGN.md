# THE PAPERS CAMPAIGN (B475) — charter

**Launched 2026-07-08 on the owner's directive: the papers must be arXiv-ready,
opponent-robust, scrutinized by our own agents before being called ready — and COMPLETE:
well-written, properly argued, philosophically grounded. The owner is a storyteller; the
writing is part of the work, not packaging.**

## The readiness definition (a paper is "ready" only when ALL hold)

1. **Verified**: every claim in the text is backed by a bank + reproducer + lock, and the
   writing pass re-ran the reproducers (promotion-time re-verification — historically this
   catches one real defect per pass; it is a feature, not overhead).
2. **Lit-gated**: every novelty-adjacent statement carries its literature verdict from the
   registry ("we could not locate", never "nobody"); Paper 4's gate = Aigner 2013,
   Reutenauer 2018, Cusick–Flahive 1989, line-by-line, plus the registry's search terms
   through the deep-research fan-out.
3. **Referee-clean**: the adversarial panel (below) has NO CONFIRMED objections standing.
4. **Complete as writing**: the paper has a narrative spine — why this question, how the
   object answers, what died on the way (the corrections ARE part of the story; this
   program's epistemology is its most distinctive literary material), what remains open,
   stated with the honesty the ledger practices. The philosophical frame is the FRAME:
   motivation and meaning around the mathematics, never inside the claims (the firewall
   holds in prose exactly as in the bank).
5. **Reproducible**: an appendix maps every result to its script and lock in the repo.
6. **The owner's voice pass**: the final text passes through the owner — drafts from the
   seats are structured FOR that pass (explicit narrative beats, marked places where the
   telling is theirs to make).

## The adversarial panel (the owner's explicit license: our own agents scrutinize first)

Run per draft, as independent agents; findings adjudicated like any incoming claim
(verify-don't-trust applies to referees too):

- **The Correctness Attacker** — re-derives every displayed formula; attacks proofs at
  their weakest quantifier; runs the reproducers cold.
- **The Novelty Attacker** — assumes every result is known; hunts prior art beyond the
  registry's terms; drafts the "this is classical because…" referee report we most fear.
- **The Overclaim Hunter** — reads only the prose: every adjective, every "therefore",
  every implicit physics suggestion; checks the firewall in every sentence.
- **The Structure/Clarity Referee** — reads as a smart non-specialist: where does the
  story lose them; what is asserted before it is motivated; what is defined after use.
- **The Hostile Physicist** — the referee who thinks the whole program is numerology;
  the draft must give them nothing to hold.

A draft advances only when each attacker's CONFIRMED findings are fixed and re-checked.
