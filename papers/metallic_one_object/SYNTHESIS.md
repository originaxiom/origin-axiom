## 1. The one object

Let $\Sigma_{1,1}$ be the once-punctured torus, $\pi_1=\langle A,B\rangle$ free of rank 2. Its mapping class
group is $\mathrm{MCG}(\Sigma_{1,1})\cong \mathrm{SL}(2,\mathbb{Z})$, generated by the two Dehn twists
$R=\left[\begin{smallmatrix}1&1\\0&1\end{smallmatrix}\right]$,
$L=\left[\begin{smallmatrix}1&0\\1&1\end{smallmatrix}\right]$. The **metallic family** is the sequence of
mapping classes
$$\varphi_m \;=\; R^m L^m \;=\; \left[\begin{smallmatrix}1+m^2 & m\\ m & 1\end{smallmatrix}\right],
\qquad \operatorname{tr}\varphi_m = m^2+2,$$
hyperbolic for all $m\ge 1$, with larger eigenvalue $\lambda_m^2$ where
$$\boxed{\;\lambda_m=\tfrac{m+\sqrt{m^2+4}}{2}\;}\quad(\text{the }m\text{-th metallic mean};\ \lambda_1=\varphi\text{ golden},\ \lambda_2=1+\sqrt2\text{ silver}).$$
$\lambda_m$ is the fundamental unit (norm $-1$) of the real quadratic field $\mathbb{Q}(\sqrt{m^2+4})$, with the
period-1 continued fraction $\lambda_m=[m;m,m,\dots]$ **[banked K002; verified this session]**. The $m=1$
member is the **figure-eight knot complement** (its once-punctured-torus bundle has monodromy
$RL=\left[\begin{smallmatrix}2&1\\1&1\end{smallmatrix}\right]$).

The single dynamical system underlying everything is the **trace map** вЂ” the $\mathrm{SL}(2,\mathbb{Z})$ action
on the Fricke character variety $(x,y,z)=(\operatorname{tr}A,\operatorname{tr}B,\operatorname{tr}AB)$ by the
Dehn twists $\tau_R,\tau_L$, preserving the **Fricke commutator invariant**
$$\kappa \;=\; \operatorname{tr}[A,B] \;=\; x^2+y^2+z^2-xyz-2 .$$
