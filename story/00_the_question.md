# 00 — The question

> Narrative, not claim (`GOVERNANCE.md`). Cites the mathematics one-way; never duplicates it.

The project begins not with a theorem but with a question, and an honest refusal to mis-ask it. Not *"why is
there something rather than nothing?"* — to *define* "nothing" is already to make it *something*. The only
well-posed version runs the other way: **"what is not-nothing?"**

That move has a consequence the project takes seriously: the minimal answers to "what is not-nothing" do not pick
out *one* object. They form a **self-generating family**. A self-referential foundation that produced a unique
seed would be the suspicious outcome — fixed points generically come in *sets*. So "we end up with a family" was
treated as the *intended shape*, not a gap to close (`../philosophy/P000`, `../philosophy/METALLIC_FOUNDATIONS.md`).

Two commitments were made here, openly, as the philosophical spine — and immediately quarantined from the
mathematics they motivate (`../philosophy/GOVERNANCE.md`): resist collapse to the trivial *maximally* (which will
point at the golden mean, the most irrational number, by Hurwitz), and generate from *self-reference*, not from
imported data. Everything mathematical that follows stands on its own without a word of this chapter.

The rest of the story is what happened when that question was handed to a concrete object and the object was made
to talk.
