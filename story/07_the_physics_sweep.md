# 07 — The physics sweep and its negatives

> Narrative, not claim. The physics chapter is **CLOSED**; every reading below is POSTULATED / firewalled to
> `../speculations/`. Cites the mathematics one-way.

The objects the program built — flat connections, a quasicrystal trace map, a knot complement — sit suggestively
close to physics, and the project ran the suggestion to ground, on a strict discipline: *compute the number, then
interpret.* The discipline held, and the answer was **robustly negative**. The negatives are the result.

The single most-leveraged computation: the volume Hessian of the metallic family is **definite `(0,2)`, not
Lorentzian** (**B96**, V80) — the Lorentzian-emergence door closed by computation, not by taste. The "tower of
spacetimes climbing the ranks" died too: the symmetric-space ladder is Lorentzian only at `k = 2` (**B101**, V85),
so there is no spacetime tower. The one place a Lorentzian form *did* appear was located honestly — the
`so(2,1)` gauge algebra of toy 2+1 Chern–Simons gravity, present *by construction*, not emergent (**B97**, V81).

Then the headline negative (**B107**, V94): the SL(2) metallic trace map **is** the Kohmoto–Kadanoff–Tang /
Fibonacci-Hamiltonian quasicrystal trace map (Sütő's invariant conserved), and every SL(3) tower eigenvalue is
`±φᵏ` — **one self-similar scale**. A fluctuation spectrum is a Hessian, generically many incommensurate scales; a
"spectrum" that collapses to one number is **re-presented moduli-space monodromy, not new physics**. The
category errors were withdrawn explicitly — *tower-eigenvalues = masses* and *torsion = masses* are **not** the
3d-3d dictionary; only the moduli-space-level `M_SUSY ≅ M_flat` (vacua = flat connections, three branches ↔ the
three fixed-point classes of chapter 06) is citable.

Out of the sweep came the one organizing insight worth keeping (`../philosophy/P001`): the framework is a theory
of **the architecture of the possible, not the furniture of the actual** — it speaks about structure (symmetry,
hierarchy, the direction of flow) and is silent about values and real-time dynamics. The brave readings, and the
walls that bound them, were banked in their own room (`../speculations/`) with an honest door — including the
held value-matches and the dead ends.
