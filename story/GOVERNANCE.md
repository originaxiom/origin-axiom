# `story/` — governance

This folder holds the **honest narrative** — how the project actually unfolded, told as a readable arc rather
than a proof. It is the room a newcomer reads first to understand *what happened and why*, before opening the
`frontier/` probes or the `V`-ledger.

> **Narrative, not claim.** Nothing here promotes to `../CLAIMS.md`. The story **cites** the mathematics (`B`-probe
> / `V`-ledger / `P`-claim numbers) so every assertion is traceable to where it is actually established — but it
> **does not duplicate the math** (no derivations, no proofs, no numbers that aren't already banked elsewhere) and
> it **never softens a negative**. When the story says something was proved, it links the `B`/`V`; when it says
> something died, it links the kill. The mathematics never cites the story.

## Rules

- **Traceability over prose.** Every factual sentence carries a `B`/`V`/`P` pointer or is plainly framed as
  narrative connective tissue. If a claim has no pointer, it is not a claim — rephrase it as story or cut it.
- **No duplicated math.** Do not restate derivations or re-prove anything; point to `frontier/` and `papers/`.
- **Negatives are part of the story.** The dead ends (the cosmological-constant value, the spacetime ladder, the
  single-scale negative) are *load-bearing chapters*, not omissions — see `../philosophy/P003`.
- **Consistent with the firewall.** Physics stays POSTULATED/CLOSED; speculation is labelled as such and lives in
  `../speculations/`; the wording rules of root `../GOVERNANCE.md` §8 bind.
