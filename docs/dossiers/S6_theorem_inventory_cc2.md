# SUMMIT DOSSIER §6 — THE THEOREM INVENTORY
### Independent pre-publication verification pass over the hearing-side and chord-side rows of `docs/LAW_MAP.md`

**Scope.** Source repo: `<cc2-seat>/origin-axiom` (READ-ONLY; nothing was modified there).
Primary document audited: `docs/LAW_MAP.md` (149 lines, sections A–E). `CLAIMS.md` was checked as the
project's formal promotion ledger — cross-referenced where relevant (none of the audited LAW_MAP rows
are yet promoted to `CLAIMS.md`; they are frontier-tier by design, so `CLAIMS.md`'s own proven/conditional
bar is not directly at stake here — this is a QA pass on the *frontier* labels themselves).

**Method.** For each row: read the LAW_MAP.md claim text and label, then opened the cited `frontier/B<N>_*`
folder(s) directly (FINDINGS.md, the verification scripts, and their output logs) to determine what evidence
actually exists — a real proof/derivation, an exhaustive-but-numeric computation, a small-sample pattern, or
a self-declared open gap — independent of how the row's own prose characterizes it. LAW_MAP's own taxonomy
(line 5–8) was used as the yardstick: **THEOREM** = proved, machine-verified; **LAW** = exact on every
*computed* instance, mechanism open/partial; **MECHANISM**; **PLACEMENT**; **WALL** = proved impossibility.
Adversarial default: a label is a MISMATCH if the underlying evidence is smaller/weaker than the word implies
— e.g., "verified to 1e-60" is NUMERICAL, not a proof, regardless of precision; "on both computed objects"
is CERTIFIED (a finite exact exhibit), not PROVED, unless a written general argument accompanies it.

**Result.** 36 rows audited (24 hearing-side, 12 chord-side, with 5 of those chord-adjacent rows drawn from
LAW_MAP §A because the task named them explicitly — sum rule, own-channel law, Jacobian reality/pairing,
sign-law family, exterior sign law). **28 KEEP, 8 DOWNGRADE.** See the closing summary for the exact list.
